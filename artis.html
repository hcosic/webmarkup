Artis The Royal Zoo

The Artis Royal Zoo, build in the 18th century, is the largest known zoo in The Netherlands. It is a famous attraction amongst tourists, the zoo is known for its diverse and large collection of animals. Besides the zoo The Artis has to offer there are many activities that Artis itself organizes for its visitors. There are space related exhibitions as well as shows in the Artis aquarium. The zoo also tends to organize events for children which makes this trip family friendly. Of course, besides the zoo you can visit there are also other things you can do in Artis as well.

Micropia

Artis-Micropia is located next to The Artis Royal Zoo in the Micropia building. Discover the world of micro-organism in this beautiful museum. Micropia offers fun and interactive experiments about this for young and adults.

Artis-Groote Museum

A brand-new museum that just got built recently. Discover about yourself and the life around you. Learn about what life looked like before humans existed and how we evolved till this day. Buy your tickets here

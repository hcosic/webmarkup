authentic restaurants

Amsterdam is a very multicultural city thus you can find authentic restaurants in The Plantage from all around the world. We recommend you to visit these restaurants during the weekdays instead the weekend, because it tends to be more busy during those days. Take away is always available!

Wertheim Park

A beautiful park full of greenery where you can relax, here you can also find the Auschwitz monument. Water within the park itself is available for free, you can fill up your water bottle during a hot sunny day.

Verzets Museum

The Dutch Resistance Museum, this is a historical museum you can visit in The Plantage. It is located just across Artis Royal Zoo. This museum shows visitors what the Netherlands looked like during World War ll and shows you the movement that existed during the war: The Resistance.
